About EasyStatisticsAcademy

EasyStatisticsAcademy is an educational platform built to teach statistics in a clear, rigorous, and beginner-friendly way—connecting fundamentals to practical analysis using SPSS, R, Python, and Excel.

Mission

To make statistics understandable and usable for beginners, applied researchers, and professionals—without sacrificing correctness. Each topic is presented with intuition, examples, and practical implementation.

Scope

The academy covers statistical foundations, probability, mathematical statistics, inference, modeling, applied data analysis, statistical software, data mining, machine learning, and AI—organized into a stable long-term structure.

Authorship

This project is maintained as a long-term academic contribution. Author information and references will be expanded over time as the site grows.
