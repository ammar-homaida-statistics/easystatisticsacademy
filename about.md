\# About EasyStatisticsAcademy



EasyStatisticsAcademy is a long-term educational platform designed to teach statistics in a clear, intuitive, and practical way.



It targets beginners, researchers, and applied users who want correct understanding and usable implementations.



\## What you will find here

\- Foundations of statistics and probability

\- Descriptive analysis and inference

\- Modeling and applied methods

\- Software implementations (SPSS, R, Python, Excel)

\- Data science topics (data mining, machine learning, AI) in an applied, statistical style



\## Project approach

The site grows gradually, focusing on correctness, clarity, and practical usefulness.
