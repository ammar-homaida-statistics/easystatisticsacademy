EasyStatisticsAcademy

EasyStatisticsAcademy is an educational platform designed to teach statistics in a simple, intuitive, and practical way.

The content is aimed at beginners, applied researchers, and non-statisticians who want to understand statistics correctly and use it with software such as SPSS, R, Python, and Excel.

What You Will Learn

  • Basic statistical concepts and intuition
  • Probability and random variables
  • Descriptive statistics
  • Statistical inference
  • Regression and applied modeling

This site is under active development.
